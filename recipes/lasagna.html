Lasagna

Description

Simple and easy to change lasagna recipe. One of the most popular dishes of all time on AllRecipes

Ingredients

  • Meat: Sweet Italian and lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato Products: Can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Spice and seasonings: Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and roctta cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
